How Transactional Only Works

Senders on this list will only have their transactional emails delivered (orders, shipping, receipts).

Marketing emails (sales, newsletters, promotions) from these senders will be automatically deleted.

To add a sender: move one of their emails to the USPIS/Transactional Only folder, or add them below.
